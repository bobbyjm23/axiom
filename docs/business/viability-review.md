## Conditions to Proceed

All conditions must be met or actively in progress before scaling beyond founding customer phase.

| # | Condition | Deadline | Owner | Status |
|---|-----------|----------|-------|--------|
| C1 | Secure **1 paid QuickStart (Adneo)** OR 1 Team Pilot | Day 90 | Founder | ⬜ Not started |
| C2 | Validate ≥3 pilot-to-production conversions | Month 12 | Founder | ⬜ Not started |
| C3 | Complete IRAP-aligned documentation (Track B prep) | Month 18 | Founder + engineer | ⬜ Not started |
| C4 | QuickStart deploy time <5 business days | Month 6 | Engineer | ⬜ Not started |

### C1 Detail: First Revenue (Track A) — Adneo-first (Thread 6)

- **Minimum:** 1 paid QuickStart — **Adneo** ($12k founding) signed + deposit received by Day 90
- **Preferred:** Adneo delivered + success report; 1+ law/accounting fit call or proposal in pipeline (outbound from wk 7)
- **Stretch (original plan):** 2 QuickStarts — defer to Month 4–5 if Adneo-first sequencing holds
- **Failure:** Zero paid engagements by Day 90 → reassess vertical (law vs accounting) or employment/consulting bridge

### C2 Detail: Conversion Validation (Track A)

- Minimum: 3 of 8 pilots convert to Team Production within 12 months
- Target: 40% conversion
- Failure: <25% after 8 pilots → reassess production pricing or pilot scope

### C3 Detail: Compliance Readiness (Deferred for Track A)

- Track A customers do not require IRAP
- Begin IRAP-aligned docs Month 12 for Track B enterprise pipeline
- Budget: ~$25k pre-seed + $90k seed (see [seed-funding-implications.md](seed-funding-implications.md))

### C4 Detail: Delivery Productisation

- QuickStart must deploy in <5 business days using standard runbook
- Failure: >65% delivery cost on QuickStart → stop custom work

## Kill Criteria

Stop or pivot if any trigger is hit. Review monthly against pipeline data.

| # | Trigger | Threshold | Measurement | Action if hit |
|---|---------|-----------|-------------|---------------|
| K1 | Zero paid pilots | 6 months of active outbound | Signed SOW count = 0 | Pivot vertical or GTM channel; if no change in 3 months, stop |
| K2 | Pilot conversion failure | <25% after 4 completed pilots | Deployments / Pilots | Reassess pilot scope, pricing, success criteria, or ICP |
| K3 | Unit economics broken | Delivery cost >70% of pilot fee | Actuals vs SOW | Raise pilot price or reduce scope; fix runbooks |
| K4 | Funding + revenue gap | Month 6 with $0 revenue and no funding | Bank balance | Bootstrap with consulting; or stop |
| K5 | Founder burnout / key-person | Unable to deliver concurrent pilots | Subjective + capacity | Hire engineer immediately or cap pipeline at 1 pilot |
