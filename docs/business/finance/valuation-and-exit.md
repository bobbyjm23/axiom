## Exit scenarios at Year 5

### 1. Strategic acquisition (most likely)

**Who buys:** AU IT services, cybersecurity, or legal/accounting tech.

| Acquirer type | Examples (illustrative) | Why they'd buy | Typical price |
|---------------|-------------------------|----------------|---------------|
| **Sovereign AI incumbent** | Cetus, Premya, Allayze | Logo base, mid-market playbook, desktop UX | $8–15M |
| **MSP / infra** | Datacom, local MSP roll-ups | Add AI deploy SKU to existing base | $6–12M |
| **Legal / prof. services tech** | Smokeball-adjacent, LEAP ecosystem | Vertical distribution | $10–20M |
| **Global AI infra** | MongoDB, Elastic, cloud vendors (stretch) | AU sovereign reference market | $15–30M |

**Deal shape:**
- **Asset sale** or **share sale** of Pty Ltd
- **Earn-out** (20–40% of price tied to 12–24 month retention / ARR)
- **Founder lock-up** 12–24 months (delivery lead or GM role)
- **Team:** 6–8 FTE transfer; redundancies rare in acqui-hires this size

**Example (base case):**
> Acquirer pays **$10M** ($7M upfront + $3M earn-out on ARR targets). Founder owns 50% → **~$3.5M cash at close** + earn-out upside.

### 2. Private equity roll-up

**Who:** AU PE rolling MSPs, compliance IT, or vertical software.

| Requirement | Y5 combo fit |
|-------------|--------------|
| $1M+ ARR | **Gap** — $580k; need scale path or wait until Y6 |
| EBITDA margin 15%+ | **Possible** Y5 if opex controlled |
| Recurring revenue | **Gap** — 32%; PE wants 50%+ |

**Verdict:** PE exit more realistic **Y6–Y7** at $1.2M+ ARR unless you accelerate Managed Lite / support-only upsells.

**Price:** 8–12× EBITDA or 3–4× revenue → **$12–18M** if metrics hit.

**When:** Y5 metrics on **scale path** ($2M+ ARR, 80+ logos) or strong growth rate.

| Round | Raise | Pre-money | Use |
|-------|-------|-----------|-----|
| Series A | $5–8M | $25–40M | National sales, Track B, ISO/IRAP, MSP channel |

**Outcome:** Build toward **$50–100M exit** in Y8–10 (category leader for AU mid-market sovereign AI).

**Founder combo path alone** may be **too small for Series A** at Y5 — unless ARR growth &gt;80% YoY and 2+ enterprise logos.
