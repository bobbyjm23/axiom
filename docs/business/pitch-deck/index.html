The Problem

Employees want AI. Compliance says no.

  • Shadow AI is everywhere — staff paste client data into ChatGPT daily
  • Public SaaS fails compliance — prompts leave Australia; Privacy Act risk
  • Copilot isn't sovereign — ~$45/user/month, US infrastructure, M365 lock-in
  • Custom builds take 6–12 months — $120k–600k before anyone logs in

Mid-market professional firms
