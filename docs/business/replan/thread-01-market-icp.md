## Ideal Customer Profile (1-Page)

### Firmographic

| Attribute | Target |
|-----------|--------|
| Employees | 50–250 (15–75 AI users in pilot) |
| Geography | Australia; capital cities + major regional |
| Revenue | $10M–$80M (est.) |
| IT maturity | 1–2 IT staff or outsourced MSP; M365 common |

### Behavioural

| Signal | Strong | Weak |
|--------|--------|------|
| Shadow AI | Known ChatGPT use with client matter data | No AI policy discussion |
| Buyer urgency | Partner / risk committee action item | "Innovation project" with no sponsor |
| Document corpus | Handbook, precedents, policies digitised | Paper-heavy, no doc owner |
| Budget | $15–40k discretionary | RFP / board-only purchases |

### Buyer personas

| Role | Concern | Message |
|------|---------|---------|
| Managing partner | Liability, reputation | Approved tool; citations from firm precedents |
| COO / practice manager | Adoption, cost | ChatGPT UX; cheaper than Copilot for pilot team |
| IT manager (if any) | Data residency | On-prem path; audit logs |
| Risk / compliance | Privacy Act, client confidentiality | Data stays on firm infra; no US SaaS |
