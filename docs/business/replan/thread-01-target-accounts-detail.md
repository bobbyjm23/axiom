## How to read this document

### AI maturity stages (market position)

| Stage | Label | Typical state | Sovereign Warden wedge |
|-------|-------|---------------|------------------------|
| **0** | Unaware | No AI policy; occasional personal ChatGPT | Policy + approved tool |
| **1** | Shadow AI | Widespread unsanctioned GenAI on client/confidential data | Urgent QuickStart after incident or audit |
| **2** | Sanctioned SaaS | Copilot, private Azure GPT, or single-vendor pilot | Governance/orchestration under existing tools |
| **3** | Firmwide legal/finance AI | Harvey, Legora, CoCounsel, Chat BDO at scale | Air-gap path, multi-tool orchestration, audit layer |
| **4** | Builder | Custom RAG, agents, proprietary platform | Infrastructure partner; compete on build-vs-buy |

### Account tiers

| Tier | Definition | Year 1 ask |
|------|------------|------------|
| **Track A** | 50–250 employees (or division-sized pilot); partner/MD budget | QuickStart $12–18k |
| **Design partner** | Top-tier / upper mid-market; warm access required | Co-develop governance layer |
| **Enterprise nurture** | 1,000+ staff; long cycle | Intel, FOMO trigger for Track A peers |
| **Intel** | Reference / investor proof only | No direct sales in Year 1 |

### Priority

| Code | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **A** | Highest close probability; fit Track A ICP |
| **B** | Design partner or secondary Track A |
| **C** | Year 2+ or intelligence only |
