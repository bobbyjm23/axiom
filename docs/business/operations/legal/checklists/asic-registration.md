## 1. Before you start

| Requirement | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| Resident director | At least one director must ordinarily reside in Australia (s201A Corporations Act 2001) |
| Director IDs | **All** directors must obtain a Director ID from ABRS **before** appointment |
| Company name | "Sovereign Warden Pty Ltd" — check availability via ASIC Business Names / company name search |
| Registered office | Must be a physical address in Australia (not a PO Box) |
