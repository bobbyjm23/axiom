# Resident Director Checklist

**DRAFT — REQUIRES AU LAWYER REVIEW BEFORE USE**

**Purpose:** Appoint an Australian resident director to satisfy s201A Corporations Act 2001 before ASIC registration.  
**Founder context:** NZ resident; cannot be sole director of an AU Pty Ltd.

---

## 1. Legal requirement

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Statute | Corporations Act 2001, s201A |
| Requirement | At least one director of a Pty Ltd must **ordinarily reside in Australia** |
| "Ordinarily resides" | Lives in Australia on a regular, settled, ongoing basis — citizenship not required |
| Non-resident founders | Allowed as additional director and/or sole shareholder |
| Director ID | All directors must have a Director ID before appointment |
| Resignation | If resident director leaves, replacement required within **28 days** + ASIC notification |

---

## 2. Options comparison

| Option | Pros | Cons | Est. cost | Priority |
|--------|------|------|-----------|----------|
| **A. Trusted AU contact** (advisor, mentor, family) | Low cost; investor-friendly; relationship trust | Personal liability on them; relationship risk | $0–500 (agreement) | **Try first** |
| **B. Professional nominee service** | Fast; experienced; handles ASIC mail | Ongoing fee; some investors scrutinise | $1,500–5,000/yr | **Fallback** |
| **C. Future AU co-founder / hire** | Long-term aligned | Not available now | Deferred | Seed stage |
| **D. Relocate to Australia** | Simplest governance | Not practical now | N/A | Future |

## 4. Governance model (recommended)

| Role | Person | Powers |
|------|--------|--------|
| Sole shareholder | Founder (100%) | Appoint/remove directors; pass shareholder resolutions |
| Non-resident director (optional) | Founder | Operational decisions at board level |
| Resident director | AU contact or nominee | ASIC/ATO correspondence; compliance seat; signs where local presence required |

**Operational control stays with founder** via shareholding and shareholder resolutions. Constitution should preserve founder's right to appoint and remove directors.

---

## 5. Risks and mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Resident director personal liability | Deed of indemnity in agreement; D&O insurance when affordable |
| Resident director resigns unexpectedly | Backup candidate identified; 28-day replacement clause |
| Investor concern about nominee | Disclose in data room; plan to replace with AU hire at seed |
| Conflict between founder and resident director | Clear written scope; founder retains 100% shares |
| Insolvent trading exposure | Resident director must be kept informed of financial position |

---
