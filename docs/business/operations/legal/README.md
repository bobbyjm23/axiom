## Disclaimer

**DRAFT — NOT LEGAL ADVICE**

All documents in this folder are **founder-drafted templates and checklists**. They are **not** a substitute for professional legal advice. Every binding instrument — especially the IP assignment deed, resident director agreement, SOW, DPA, and SAFE — **must be reviewed by an AU corporate/commercial lawyer** before signing.
