## For angel bridge (SAFE only)

SAFE investors typically do **not** require a shareholders agreement. Their rights are in the SAFE instrument:

- Conversion at cap
- Pro-rata (if negotiated)
- MFN clause
- Information rights

If a lead angel requests a shareholders agreement, scope it narrowly to investor protections — do not give operational control.
