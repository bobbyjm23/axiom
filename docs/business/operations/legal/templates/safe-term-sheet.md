## 2. Valuation cap

2.1 The Investment Amount will convert into ordinary shares at the Valuation Cap of **$[2,500,000 / 2,000,000]** (post-money) upon a Qualifying Event.

2.2 The applicable cap depends on whether the Investor meets the Intro Threshold (Section 7).

---

## 3. Conversion events

The SAFE converts into ordinary shares upon the earliest of:

| Event | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Equity financing** | Company sells preferred shares in a bona fide round of >$500k |
| **Liquidity event** | Merger, acquisition, IPO, or asset sale |
| **Dissolution** | Company winds up (investor receives return of Investment Amount before ordinary shareholders) |

---

## 4. Conversion mechanics

4.1 On conversion at an equity financing, the Investment Amount converts at the lower of:

(a) the Valuation Cap; or

(b) the price per share in the equity financing.

4.2 Number of shares = Investment Amount ÷ conversion price.

4.3 Fractional shares will be rounded down.

---

## 5. Company representations

The Company represents that:

(a) it is duly incorporated under the laws of Australia;

(b) the SAFE has been duly authorised by the board and shareholders;

(c) all intellectual property is owned by or licensed to the Company (IP assignment deed executed);

(d) there are no outstanding convertible instruments other than SAFEs in this round; and

(e) the founder has disclosed any employment conflicts of interest.

---

## 6. Investor representations

The Investor represents that:

(a) they are an experienced or sophisticated investor (s708 Corporations Act);

(b) they are investing for their own account, not for redistribution;

(c) they understand the SAFE may never convert and they may lose their entire investment; and

(d) they have had the opportunity to ask questions and review the Company's materials.

---

## 7. Intro threshold (reduced cap)

7.1 If the Investor introduces **two (2) or more** qualified buyer meetings with law firms or accounting firms in the Company's target market (50–250 employees, Australia) within [6] months of investment, the Valuation Cap is reduced to **$2,000,000**.

7.2 "Qualified" means: decision-maker meeting, relevant to the Company's ICP, and not already in the Company's pipeline.

---

## 8. Information rights

8.1 The Company will provide quarterly updates to Investors (revenue, pipeline, runway, key metrics).

8.2 No audit or inspection rights unless Investor holds >10% on conversion.

---

## 9. Most favoured nation (MFN)

9.1 If the Company issues SAFEs with more favourable terms before the next equity financing, this SAFE will be amended to match those terms (at Investor's election).
