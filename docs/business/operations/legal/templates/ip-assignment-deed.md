## Recitals

A. The Assignor is the sole shareholder of the Company.

B. The Assignor has developed certain intellectual property relating to the "Sovereign Warden" sovereign AI platform, including software, documentation, brand assets, and related materials.

C. The Assignor wishes to assign all right, title, and interest in the Assigned IP to the Company.

D. The Company wishes to accept the assignment.

E. The Assignor confirms that no Sovereign Warden intellectual property has been assigned to any other entity, including any New Zealand trading company.

---

## 1. Definitions

**"Assigned IP"** means all intellectual property described in Schedule 1, and includes:

(a) all copyright, patent rights, trade mark rights, design rights, and trade secret rights;

(b) all rights to sue for past infringement;

(c) all renewals, extensions, and reversions of the above; and

(d) all applications and rights to apply for registration of the above.

**"Moral Rights"** has the meaning given in the Copyright Act 1968 (Cth).

---

## 2. Assignment

2.1 The Assignor hereby assigns to the Company all right, title, and interest in the Assigned IP, effective from **[EFFECTIVE_DATE]**.

2.2 The assignment is worldwide and perpetual.

2.3 The consideration for this assignment is the issue of [NUMBER] ordinary shares in the Company to the Assignor [already issued / on the date of this deed].

---

## 3. Moral rights

3.1 To the extent permitted by law, the Assignor consents to all acts by the Company that would otherwise infringe the Assignor's Moral Rights in the Assigned IP.

3.2 The Assignor will not assert Moral Rights against the Company or its licensees in respect of the Assigned IP.

---

## 4. Warranties

The Assignor warrants that:

(a) the Assignor is the sole owner of the Assigned IP;

(b) the Assigned IP is not subject to any mortgage, charge, lien, or third-party claim;

(c) the Assigned IP has not been assigned to any other person or entity (including any New Zealand company);

(d) the Assignor has the full right and authority to assign the Assigned IP;

(e) to the best of the Assignor's knowledge, the Assigned IP does not infringe any third-party intellectual property rights;

(f) the Assignor has disclosed all third-party open-source components incorporated in the software (see Schedule 2).

---

## 5. Further assurances

5.1 The Assignor will, at the Company's expense, do all things and execute all documents reasonably required to give effect to this assignment, including:

(a) assisting with trade mark applications in the Company's name;

(b) transferring domain name registrations;

(c) signing patent or design applications (if any); and

(d) providing evidence of authorship and creation dates.
