# Contractor IP Assignment Clause

**DRAFT — REQUIRES AU LAWYER REVIEW BEFORE USE**

**Purpose:** Insert into contractor agreements, consulting agreements, and offer letters for delivery engineers and advisors who create IP for Sovereign Warden.

---

## Clause (for insertion into contractor agreement)

### Intellectual Property

1. **Ownership.** The Contractor agrees that all intellectual property created, developed, or reduced to practice by the Contractor, alone or with others, in connection with the services or the business of Sovereign Warden Pty Ltd ("the Company"), including but not limited to software, documentation, designs, inventions, and know-how ("Work Product"), is the sole and exclusive property of the Company.

2. **Assignment.** The Contractor hereby assigns to the Company all right, title, and interest in the Work Product, including all copyright, patent, trade mark, and trade secret rights, effective upon creation.

3. **Moral rights.** To the extent permitted by law, the Contractor consents to all acts by the Company that would otherwise infringe the Contractor's moral rights in the Work Product.

4. **Prior IP.** The Contractor will disclose any pre-existing intellectual property ("Prior IP") incorporated into the Work Product. The Contractor grants the Company a perpetual, royalty-free licence to use Prior IP as part of the Work Product.

5. **Further assurances.** The Contractor will execute documents and take actions reasonably required to give effect to this assignment, at the Company's expense.

6. **No conflicting obligations.** The Contractor warrants that the Work Product does not infringe third-party rights and that the Contractor is not bound by any agreement that would prevent assignment to the Company.
