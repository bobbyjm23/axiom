# Constitution Notes

**DRAFT — REQUIRES AU LAWYER REVIEW BEFORE USE**

**Purpose:** Guidance for adopting a constitution for Sovereign Warden Pty Ltd. Discuss options with your AU corporate lawyer before registration.

---

## 1. Options

| Option | Description | Cost | When to use |
|--------|-------------|------|-------------|
| **Replaceable rules** | Default rules under Corporations Act 2001; no custom document needed | $0 | Fastest registration; sufficient for solo founder at formation |
| **Custom constitution** | Tailored document governing shareholder and director rights | $500–1,500 (lawyer) | Before angel SAFE or if specific provisions needed |

**Recommendation:** Register with **replaceable rules** for speed. Adopt a custom constitution (or amend) before the angel bridge SAFE raise if investors require specific provisions.

---

## 2. Key provisions to discuss with lawyer

### For solo founder (formation)

| Provision | Recommendation |
|-----------|----------------|
| Director appointment | Shareholders may appoint/remove directors by ordinary resolution |
| Share classes | Single class of ordinary shares |
| Transfer restrictions | Consider drag-along/tag-along for future investors |
| Dividend policy | Discretionary — no mandatory dividends |

### For angel bridge (before SAFE)

| Provision | Recommendation |
|-----------|----------------|
| Share issue authority | Board authorised to issue shares up to [SAFE_AMOUNT] under SAFE conversions |
| Pre-emptive rights | Standard — new shares offered to existing shareholders first |
| Preference shares | Not needed for SAFE (converts to ordinary) |
| Investor observer rights | Optional — angel may request board observer seat (non-voting) |

### For future co-founder

| Provision | Recommendation |
|-----------|----------------|
| Vesting | Standard 4-year vesting with 1-year cliff |
| Good leaver / bad leaver | Define repurchase price for unvested shares |
| Decision-making | Reserved matters requiring unanimous or supermajority consent |

---

## 3. Replaceable rules — key defaults

If you use replaceable rules (no custom constitution), the following defaults apply under the Corporations Act:

- Directors are appointed by shareholder resolution
- Each share carries one vote
- Directors may issue shares with shareholder approval (or within authorised capital)
- Annual general meeting required if >1 shareholder (not applicable at formation)
- Financial reports required per size thresholds

---

## 4. Investor-friendly provisions (for SAFE conversion)

When preparing for angel bridge, consider adding:

| Provision | Why |
|-----------|-----|
| Authorised share capital | Sufficient ordinary shares for SAFE conversion without repeated shareholder resolutions |
| Board composition | Founder remains sole director (or adds resident director); angel may request observer |
| Information rights | Quarterly financial updates to investors holding >[5]% |
| Anti-dilution | SAFE handles this at instrument level — no need in constitution for post-money SAFE |

## 5. Resident director considerations

| Item | Note |
|------|------|
| Director removal | Founder (sole shareholder) can remove resident director by resolution |
| Quorum | With 1–2 directors, specify quorum (typically 1 or 2) |
| Meeting format | Allow virtual meetings (video conference) |
