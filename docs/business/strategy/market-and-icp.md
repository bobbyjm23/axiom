# Market and ICP

**Target:** Australian regulated mid-market · 50–250 employees  
**Primary vertical:** Mid-tier law (50–150 lawyers)  
**Secondary:** Accounting and advisory

---

## Market size

| Layer | Estimate |
|-------|----------|
| **SAM (Track A)** | ~10,000–17,000 regulated knowledge-work orgs |
| **SAM (law subset)** | ~800–1,200 mid-tier law firms |
| **SOM (Year 3)** | 15–25 active customers; 40–60 cumulative logos |
| **Sovereign AI adoption today** | &lt;5% in mid-tier law (mostly shadow ChatGPT + Copilot trials) |

---

## Ideal customer profile

| Attribute | Target |
|-----------|--------|
| Employees | 50–250 (15–75 AI users in pilot) |
| Geography | Australia |
| Revenue | ~$10M–$80M |
| IT | 1–2 IT staff or outsourced MSP; M365 common |
| Budget authority | $15–40k without board (QuickStart / pilot) |

### Strong buying signals

- Known ChatGPT use with client or confidential data
- Partner or risk committee mandate after incident or audit
- Digitised handbook, precedents, policies
- Copilot quote shock for 50+ seats

### Excluded (Year 1)

Big 4, federal government, defence, ASX 50, firms &lt;20 employees.

---

## Buyer personas

| Role | Cares about | Message |
|------|-------------|---------|
| Managing partner | Liability, reputation | Approved tool with citations from firm documents |
| COO / practice manager | Adoption, cost | ChatGPT UX; cheaper than Copilot for pilot team |
| IT manager | Data residency | AU-hosted or on-prem path; audit logs |
| Risk / compliance | Privacy Act | Data control; no default US inference |

---

## Trigger events

| Trigger | Buy window |
|---------|------------|
| Shadow ChatGPT on client matter | 14–60 days |
| Privacy / risk audit flags unapproved AI | 30–90 days |
| Copilot quote for 50+ users | 60–120 days |
| Competitor firm announces AI initiative | 90–180 days (FOMO after case studies) |
