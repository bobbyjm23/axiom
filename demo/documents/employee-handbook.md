# Sovereign Warden — Employee Handbook (Demo Document)

## Remote Work Policy

All full-time employees may work remotely up to **3 days per week** with manager approval. Remote work arrangements must be documented in the HR system within 30 days of starting.

Employees working remotely must:
- Maintain a dedicated workspace with reliable internet (minimum 25 Mbps)
- Be available during core hours: 10:00 AM – 3:00 PM local time
- Attend mandatory in-office days on the first Tuesday of each month

## Annual Leave

Employees accrue **20 days of annual leave** per calendar year, pro-rated for part-time staff. Leave requests require 2 weeks notice for periods exceeding 5 consecutive days.

Unused leave may be carried forward up to **5 days** into the following year. Leave balances are visible in the employee self-service portal.

## Security Requirements

All employees must:
- Enable multi-factor authentication on corporate accounts
- Use the company VPN when accessing internal systems from public networks
- Report security incidents within 1 hour to security@company.internal

Company data must not be stored on personal devices or uploaded to unauthorized cloud services. Use approved platforms only.

## AI Tool Usage

Employees may use the **Sovereign Warden Platform** for work-related tasks. Do not paste customer PII, unreleased financial data, or credentials into AI chat interfaces.

All AI interactions are logged for compliance. Contact IT for access requests.
