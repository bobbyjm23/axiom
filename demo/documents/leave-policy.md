# Leave and Benefits Policy (Demo Document)

## Annual Leave Entitlement

| Employment type | Annual leave | Sick leave |
|-----------------|-------------|------------|
| Full-time | 20 days | 10 days |
| Part-time (50%+) | Pro-rated | Pro-rated |
| Contract (< 12 months) | Pro-rated | 5 days |

## Public Holidays

Employees receive **10 public holidays** per year in addition to annual leave. A substitute day policy applies when holidays fall on weekends.

## Parental Leave

Primary caregivers receive **16 weeks paid parental leave**. Secondary caregivers receive **4 weeks paid leave**. Applications must be submitted 8 weeks before the expected start date.

## Professional Development

Each employee has an annual **$2,000 learning budget** for courses, conferences, and certifications. Pre-approval from your manager is required for expenses over $500.

## Health and Wellbeing

The company provides:
- Employee assistance program (EAP) — confidential counseling
- Subsidized gym membership (up to $80/month)
- Mental health days — 2 additional days per year, no medical certificate required

## Questions

Contact HR at hr@company.internal or visit the employee self-service portal.
