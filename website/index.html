The challenge

Employees want AI. Compliance says no.

Australian regulated firms face a structural conflict: staff paste client data into ChatGPT while boards mandate data control. Microsoft Copilot offers familiarity but routes prompts through US infrastructure at ~$45–50 per user per month. Competitors like Premya and Cetus sell sovereignty — but often at enterprise timelines and opaque pricing.

  • Shadow AI risk

    Unsanctioned tools create Privacy Act exposure and no audit trail. You need an approved assistant employees will actually use.

    Read our guide →

  • Copilot economics

    Per-seat cloud AI compounds at scale. On-premises open-weight models on owned hardware break even in months — with full sovereignty.

    TCO comparison →

  • Implementation gap

    Custom SI builds take 12–24 weeks with no adoption guarantee. API-only tools fail with lawyers, accountants, and advisers who need a familiar chat interface.
